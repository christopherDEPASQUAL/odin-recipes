Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an iconic dish of Italian cuisine, loved around the world for its richness and flavor. This savory dish is made up of alternating layers of pasta, sauces and toppings, all carefully assembled to create a comforting and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 green pepper, chopped
  • 1 (28 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (6 ounce) can tomato paste

Step for kitchen

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet over medium heat, cook beef, onion and green pepper until meat is browned and vegetables are tender. Drain excess fat. Stir in tomato sauce and simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Spread 1/2 cup of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Arrange 1/2 of the cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread 1/2 of the ricotta cheese over the noodles, then top with 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers.
  5. Bake in preheated oven for 25 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.

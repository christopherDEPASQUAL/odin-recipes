Slow Cooker Pulled Pork

Description

Slow Cooker Pulled Pork is a delicious and easy recipe that is perfect for any occasion. The pork is cooked low and slow in the slow cooker, resulting in tender and juicy meat that falls apart easily. The pork is then shredded and served with your favorite barbecue sauce. This recipe is sure to be a hit with your family and friends!

Ingredients

  • 1 (4-5 pound) pork shoulder roast
  • 1 (12 ounce) bottle barbecue sauce
  • 1 cup root beer, cola, or ginger ale
  • 1 large onion, sliced
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground mustard
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Optional: 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Place the sliced onion and minced garlic at the bottom of the slow cooker.
  3. Rub the pork shoulder roast with smoked paprika, brown sugar, ground mustard, chili powder, salt, and pepper.
  4. Place the seasoned pork shoulder on top of the onions and garlic in the slow cooker.
  5. Pour 1 cup of your chosen soda (root beer, cola, or ginger ale) over the pork.
  6. Cover and cook on low for 8-10 hours or until the pork is tender and easily shredded with a fork.
  7. Once cooked, remove the pork from the slow cooker and shred it using two forks.
  8. Drain the excess liquid from the slow cooker, reserving about 1 cup.
  9. Return the shredded pork to the slow cooker, add the barbecue sauce, and mix well. If needed, add some of the reserved cooking liquid to keep the pork moist.
  10. Cover and cook on low for an additional 30 minutes to 1 hour to allow the flavors to meld.
  11. Serve the pulled pork on buns with coleslaw, or use it as a filling for tacos, sandwiches, or over rice.
